# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

**DRAFT - FOR INTERNAL REVIEW ONLY**

*Last Updated: [DATE]*

---

## 1. Definitions

- **"Controller"**: Customer (the entity using Lineup)
- **"Processor"**: Lineup (provider of the Service)
- **"Personal Data"**: As defined in GDPR/applicable law
- **"Processing"**: Any operation on Personal Data

---

## 2. Scope

This DPA applies to Personal Data processed by Lineup on behalf of Customer in connection with the Service.

---

## 3. Data Processing Details

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Subject Matter** | Interview management and scheduling |
| **Duration** | Term of the Service Agreement |
| **Nature & Purpose** | Store and process candidate/user data for scheduling |
| **Data Types** | Names, emails, phone numbers, interview data |
| **Data Subjects** | Candidates, recruiters, interviewers |

---

## 4. Processor Obligations

Lineup shall:

1. Process data only on documented instructions
2. Ensure personnel confidentiality
3. Implement appropriate security measures
4. Assist with data subject requests
5. Assist with DPIAs if required
6. Delete or return data upon termination
7. Make available information for audits
8. Notify of data breaches without undue delay

---

## 5. Sub-Processors

| Name | Purpose | Location |

| AWS | Cloud hosting | US/EU |

### Sub-Processor Changes

- We will notify Customer of new sub-processors
- Customer may object within 14 days
- If objection not resolved, Customer may terminate

---

## 6. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Employee security training
- Incident response procedures
- Business continuity measures

---

## 7. International Transfers

For transfers outside EEA/UK:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2: Controller to Processor)
- Supplementary measures as needed
- Data residency options available (Enterprise)

---

## 8. Data Subject Rights

We will:

- Assist Customer in responding to data subject requests
- Provide tools for data export and deletion
- Not respond directly to data subjects without authorization

---

## 9. Data Breach Notification

We will notify Customer of Personal Data breaches:

- Without undue delay (within 72 hours)
- Including nature, categories, consequences
- Including mitigation measures taken

---

## 10. Audits

- Customer may audit compliance (reasonable notice)
- We provide third-party audit reports
- On-site audits arranged at Customer cost

---

## 11. Termination

Upon termination:

- Data export available for 30 days
- Data deleted within 90 days
- Deletion confirmation available on request

---

## 12. Liability

DPA liability follows main Service Agreement terms.

---

## 13. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the same law as the main Service Agreement.
